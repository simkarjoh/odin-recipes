Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and a blend of cheeses. To prepare it, start by cooking a hearty meat sauce with ground beef or sausage, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and Italian herbs. In a separate pan, prepare a smooth béchamel sauce by whisking butter, flour, and milk until thickened. Begin assembling by spreading a layer of the meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of béchamel, and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with a final layer of sauce and cheese on top. Bake the lasagna in the oven until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown, then let it rest for a few minutes before slicing into it. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil or parsley.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Italian seasoning
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef or sausage to the skillet, and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, eggs, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
